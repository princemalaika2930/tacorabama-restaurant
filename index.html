Our Story

The Tacorabama Fusion Experience

Born from a passion for bold flavors and culinary creativity, Tacorabama Pizza House brings together the best of Southern, African, and Italian cuisines. Our founder, Chef Kwame Johnson, grew up with Southern roots and West African heritage, creating a unique fusion that celebrates both cultures.

At Tacorabama, we specialize in authentic African dishes like jollof rice, fried rice with unique toppings, wood-fired pizzas with unexpected flavors, and tacos with a Southern twist. Our bar features craft cocktails that perfectly complement our fusion dishes.

Every dish is prepared with locally-sourced ingredients and traditional techniques, ensuring an unforgettable dining experience that tantalizes your taste buds with every bite.
